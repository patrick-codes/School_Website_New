The school acknowledges all important events that help to make us distinctive through the observance of the following:

  • Founder’s Day – 30th January
  • Appreciation Day – 14th February
  • Prayer session with parents of Finalists – Last Sunday before B.E.C.E
  • African Union Day – 25th May
  • Induction Service for New Staff and New Pupils at the beginning of a new academic year
  • Funfair with parents – Yearly
  • End of Year carols Service – In December
  • Annual Staff and Management Seminar (SMS): Yearly.
  • Happy Kids got talent – Last Saturday of the 2nd term
